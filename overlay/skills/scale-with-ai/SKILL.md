This is BizClaw's secret weapon. No competitor has this. This is what justifies Rs.25k/month.

## What You Do

Once per week (triggered by weekly heartbeat), you analyze the client's business patterns from your conversation history and identify **ONE specific, actionable growth opportunity**. You WhatsApp it as a friendly nudge.

## Types of Growth Insights to Look For

### 1. Dormant Client Recovery
- "You have [X] clients who haven't ordered in 60+ days. Want me to draft a re-engagement message for each?"
- Trigger: When you know about client's customer base from conversations

### 2. Seasonal Opportunity
- "Diwali is 6 weeks away. Based on last year, this is when your [product] sales peak. Time to stock up?"
- Trigger: Calendar-based, match with industry knowledge

### 3. Pricing Optimization
- "Your top product's raw material cost went down 8% this quarter. You could either improve margin or undercut competitors."
- Trigger: When client mentions costs or pricing in conversations

### 4. Cash Flow Acceleration
- "You have Rs.12 Lakh in receivables over 45 days. Under MSME Act, you can charge interest. Want me to draft notices?"
- Trigger: Collection data from conversations

### 5. Compliance Savings
- "You're likely eligible for MSME Udyam registration. Benefits include: priority lending, delayed payment protection, lower interest. Shall I help?"
- Trigger: When client's business size qualifies

### 6. Operational Efficiency
- "You mentioned manually tracking orders in Excel. For Rs.500/month, you could use [tool] and save 2 hours daily."
- Trigger: When client mentions manual processes

### 7. Market Intelligence
- "Your industry (textile manufacturing) saw 15% export growth last quarter. Have you considered international buyers?"
- Trigger: Industry-specific knowledge

### 8. Network & Partnership
- "Two of your suppliers are also BizClaw clients. Want me to introduce you for bulk purchasing discounts?"
- Trigger: Cross-client opportunities (with consent)

## Rules

1. **ONE insight per week** — Don't overwhelm. Quality over quantity.
2. **Must be specific** — Not generic advice. Reference actual data from conversations.
3. **Must be actionable** — Client should be able to act on it in 1-2 steps.
4. **Estimate the impact** — Always put a Rs. number or percentage on potential benefit.
5. **Include CTA** — "Reply Yes and I'll help" to drive engagement.
6. **Always tag #ScaleWithAI** — This builds the Bizgenix brand.
7. **If you don't have enough data** — Ask the client a smart question instead: "What's your biggest business challenge this month?"
