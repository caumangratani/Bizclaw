# Friend Reminders — WhatsApp Messages on Your Behalf

Business owners hate awkward "paisa kab doge" conversations. BizClaw sends polite, professional reminders to their clients and vendors directly on WhatsApp — maintaining relationships while getting the job done.

## How It Works

1. Owner says: "Send reminder to Sharma for 50000 payment"
2. BizClaw drafts a polite message
3. Shows preview to owner for approval
4. Owner says "send" → message delivered via WhatsApp
5. Tracks delivery and response

**CRITICAL RULE: NEVER send a message without owner's explicit approval. Always show preview first.**

## Rules

1. **NEVER send without explicit owner approval** — always show preview first
2. Keep messages polite and professional — these are real business relationships
3. Include owner's name and business name in every message
4. Respect timing — don't send before 9 AM or after 8 PM IST
5. Don't send more than 1 reminder per party per week (unless owner insists)
6. Track delivery status and notify owner of replies
7. Support English, Hindi, Gujarati — match the language the owner uses with that party
8. For bulk sends, add 30-second gap between messages (avoid WhatsApp flagging)
9. Never threaten or use aggressive language — even for 90+ day overdue
