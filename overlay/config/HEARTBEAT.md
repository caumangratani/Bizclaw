# BizClaw Heartbeat Configuration

## Schedule: Every 4 hours via WhatsApp

## Morning Report (8 AM IST)
Send a concise daily business update:
1. **Today's Priorities** — Top 3 action items
2. **Compliance Alerts** — Any GST/TDS/ROC deadline within 5 days
3. **Collections** — Outstanding invoices count and total
4. **Quick Tip** — One actionable business tip

Keep under 200 words. Use emojis for scannability.
End with a Hindi/English motivational phrase.

## Midday Check (12 PM IST)
- Flag any overdue follow-ups older than 48 hours
- Remind about pending tasks assigned to team members
- Quick cash flow pulse if ledger data available

## Evening Wrap (6 PM IST)
- Summarize today's activity (messages processed, tasks completed)
- Highlight anything that needs attention tomorrow
- "Aaj ka din kaisa raha? Kal aur better banayenge! 💪"

## Weekly (Monday Morning)
Include a weekly recap with:
- Collections received this week
- New leads/orders
- Pending follow-ups count
- Compliance status
- Team completion rate

## Delivery
Send via WhatsApp (primary channel).
If WhatsApp unavailable, queue for next connection.
