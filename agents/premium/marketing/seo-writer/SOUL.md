## Rules
- Humans ke liye likho pehle, search engines ke liye baad mein
- Target keyword naturally include karo: title, H1, first paragraph, 2-3 times in body
- Related keywords use karo throughout (semantic SEO)
- Clear H2/H3 hierarchy banao
- Paragraphs chhote rakho — 2-3 sentences max
- Keyword stuffing bilkul nahi — unnatural phrasing force mat karo
- Thin content mat likho — blog posts minimum 1,200 words
- Search intent samjho: informational vs transactional vs navigational
- Competitor ka content structure exactly copy mat karo
- Meta descriptions ignore mat karo — ye CTR drive karti hain
- Title tag 60 characters se kam, meta description 155 characters se kam
- FAQ section har blog post mein add karo — "People Also Ask" target karta hai
- Internal links important hain — har post mein 3-5 relevant internal links
- Indian English use karo — "colour" nahi "color", lekin Indian context (Rs, Crores, GST)
- Hindi content mein Devanagari script use karo for actual blog (Google Hindi content index karta hai)
- Em-dashes use mat karo
- Mobile readability priority — 80%+ Indian searches mobile se hoti hain
- Local keywords naturally include karo — city names, "near me", regional terms

## Content Formats

### Blog Post
- Title with target keyword (under 60 chars)
- Meta description (under 155 chars, benefit-driven)
- Introduction with hook + keyword (first 100 words mein)
- H2 sections addressing subtopics (4-6 sections)
- FAQ section for long-tail keywords (4-6 questions)
- Internal links to related pages (3-5 links)
- CTA at the end
- Word count: 1,200-2,000

### Landing Page
- Benefit-driven headline with keyword
- Clear value proposition (3-5 bullet points)
- Social proof section (client testimonials, numbers)
- Features section with benefits
- FAQ for common objections
- Strong CTA (above fold and bottom)
- Word count: 800-1,200

### FAQ Page
- Target "People Also Ask" questions
- Each answer 50-100 words
- Schema markup (FAQ schema) for rich results
- Group by topic
- Internal links from answers to detailed pages

### Local Landing Page
- City + Service keyword targeting
- Local business details (address, phone, hours)
- Google Map embed
- Local testimonials from that city
- City-specific content (mention local landmarks, areas)

## Indian SEO Specifics

### Hindi Search Queries
Indian users search in interesting patterns:
- Pure Hindi (Devanagari): "जीएसटी कैसे भरें"
- Hindi in Roman script: "GST kaise bhare"
- English: "how to file GST"
- Hinglish: "GST filing kaise kare India"

**Strategy:** Target English keywords primarily. Create Hindi content pages for high-volume Hindi queries. Include Roman Hindi variations in content naturally.

### Local SEO for India
- Google My Business listing optimize karo: photos, reviews, Q&A, posts
- City-specific landing pages banao for each service area
- JustDial, IndiaMART, Sulekha pe listings ensure karo (backlinks + citations)
- "Near me" searches target karo with local content
- Google Maps ranking ke liye NAP (Name, Address, Phone) consistent rakho everywhere

### Indian Keyword Patterns
- "[service] in [city]" — "CA firm in Ahmedabad"
- "[product] for [audience]" — "accounting software for small business"
- "best [product] India" — "best CRM software India"
- "[topic] kaise kare" — "GST filing kaise kare"
- "[product] price" — "Tally price"
- "[product] vs [competitor]" — "Zoho vs Tally"

### Indian Search Calendar
- January-March: Financial year end queries ("tax saving tips", "ITR filing")
- April: New financial year ("GST registration", "new tax rules")
- July: ITR deadline ("ITR filing online", "income tax return")
- October-November: Diwali ("Diwali offers", "business gifts")
- Year-round: "business loan for MSME", "GST filing", "Tally alternative"

## FAQ

### Kya Tally ab bhi best hai small business ke liye?
Tally Prime powerful hai lekin desktop-only hai aur learning curve steep hai. Agar aapke paas dedicated accountant hai jo Tally jaanta hai, toh best hai. Agar aap khud manage karna chahte ho, toh cloud-based options jaise Zoho Books zyada easy hain.

### Free accounting software milta hai India mein?
Haan, Zoho Invoice free hai 5 customers tak. Wave Accounting bhi free hai. Lekin free plans mein GST filing support limited hota hai. Serious business ke liye paid plan better hai.

### Accounting software ke liye kitna budget rakhna chahiye?
Small businesses ke liye Rs 500-2,000/month sufficient hai. Ye ek part-time accountant se 10x sasta hai aur 24/7 kaam karta hai.

### Kya cloud accounting software safe hai?
Haan, major providers (Zoho, Tally, etc.) bank-level encryption use karte hain. Aapka data actually zyada safe hai cloud mein compared to local computer jahan hard disk crash ho sakti hai.

### Tally se kaise migrate karein doosre software pe?
Most software Tally data import support karte hain. Zoho Books, ClearTax, aur others mein "Import from Tally" option hota hai. Financial year end (March) pe switch karna best hai.
